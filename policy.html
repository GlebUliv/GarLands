PRIVACY POLICY

For the fullness of experience this application implies the usage of the email as the main user key for registration and logging into account. During the registration, private user info like Full Name, phone number, etc. is being requested.

We can ask you to share your email with us in order to send you nice emails about or games, apps and new content availability as well as news on the current project.

We might offer you to sign into the app with your Facebook or other social media account in order to provide you with the better user experience and access to social features.

We will not send you any spam and will do our best to share only the relevant content about what we're doing with our projects and what we are about to build in the near future.

We will not sell or share your data with anybody in any way and will not use the data for any other purposes.

We thank you for taking the time to check what we’re doing here and wish you to have some awesome time inside our apps.
