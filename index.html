GARLands is a new way to enjoy the good old decorative paper festoons in Augmented Reality.

Allow the app to detect the walls and environment, then place the anchor points to put up the festoon of your choice and see how it looks inside the room. Take photos and selfies with the carefully crafted festoons from our collections. Make your holidays more fun!

Every placed festoon is measured for an actual physical length and this information can be used in the future to produce or print the real festoons.
